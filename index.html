Welcome! I am Hui Yuan [xweɪ ɥæn] and my name in Chinese is 袁慧. I'm currently a final-year ECE PhD student at Princeton University, fortunately advised by Mengdi Wang. Prior to Princeton, I got my Bachelor’s degree in Statistics from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2020. My research interests lie in the foundation of machine learning methods and their applications to real-world decision making.

In the past few years, I'm also fortunate to work with Yinyu Ye, Csaba Szepesvári and Yingyu Liang. In summer 2024, I interned at Meta.
